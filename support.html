Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan bills?

To scan a bill, simply tap the camera button in the app and point your camera at the bill. Our smart OCR technology will automatically detect and extract important information.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use bank-level encryption to protect your data. Bills stored in the vault are additionally encrypted with your personal PIN.

How does iCloud sync work?

iCloud sync automatically keeps your bills and categories in sync across all your iOS devices. Just make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID and have iCloud enabled.

Can I export my bills?

Yes, you can export individual bills or multiple bills as PDF files. You can also share bills directly from the app.

How do I restore my purchase?

To restore your purchase, go to the Settings screen and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

What happens if I forget my vault PIN?

For security reasons, vault PINs cannot be recovered. You'll need to reset the vault, which will remove the encryption from your bills.
