Support Center

We're here to help you get the most out of Billify

Common Issues

Here are solutions to frequently encountered issues:

  • If the app is running slowly, try closing other apps or restarting your device
  • For scanning issues, ensure good lighting and a clear background
  • If iCloud sync isn't working, check your internet connection and iCloud settings
  • To improve OCR accuracy, hold your device steady while scanning

Data Management

Important information about your data:

  • Regular backups are recommended
  • Data is stored locally and in your personal iCloud (if enabled)
  • Export important documents regularly as PDFs
  • Check scanned information for accuracy

Best Practices

  • Organize bills into relevant categories
  • Use good lighting when scanning documents
  • Keep your app updated to the latest version
  • Review scanned data before saving
