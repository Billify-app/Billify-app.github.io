Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Zero Data Collection Policy

Billify does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. All your information stays locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account if you choose to enable iCloud sync.

Introduction

At Billify, we take your privacy seriously. Our app is designed with a "privacy-first" approach, ensuring that all your data remains under your complete control.

Local Data Storage

Here's how your data is handled:

  • All bill images and data are stored only on your device
  • If you enable iCloud sync, data is stored in your personal iCloud account
  • We have zero access to your data
  • No data is ever transmitted to our servers
  • No analytics or tracking is implemented

App Permissions

Billify requires only essential permissions to function:

  • Camera: Only for scanning bills (when you explicitly use the scanner)
  • Photo Library: Only for importing bill images (when you choose to import)
  • iCloud: Only if you enable sync (optional)

In-App Purchases

For in-app purchases:

  • All transactions are handled directly by Apple
  • We never have access to your payment information
  • Purchase status is stored locally on your device

Data Security

Your security is ensured through:

  • Local device encryption
  • Secure vault feature with PIN protection
  • iCloud's built-in security (if sync is enabled)

Your Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Add or delete bills at any time
  • Enable or disable iCloud sync
  • Export your data in PDF format
  • Completely remove all data by deleting the app

Children's Privacy

Billify does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at:

app.billify@gmail.com

This privacy policy is subject to change. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.
