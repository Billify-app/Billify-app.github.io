Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Billify's Privacy Policy. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Bill images and data you scan
  • Categories and tags you create
  • Device information for app functionality
  • Usage data to improve our service

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process and store your bills
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Provide customer support

Data Security and Storage

Important information about your data:

  • Your data is stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account (if enabled)
  • We do not store your data on our servers
  • While we implement standard security measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security
  • You are responsible for maintaining your own backups
  • We are not liable for any data loss that may occur

Data Accuracy

Regarding our OCR and scanning features:

  • The accuracy of text recognition may vary
  • You should always verify the scanned information
  • We do not guarantee 100% accuracy of automated features
  • You are responsible for checking and correcting any errors

Your Responsibilities

As a user, you are responsible for:

  • Maintaining backups of your important documents
  • Verifying the accuracy of scanned information
  • Keeping your device and account secure
  • Complying with applicable laws regarding document storage

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your data from the app
  • Opt-out of analytics
  • Export your data (in PDF format)

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

app.billify@gmail.com
