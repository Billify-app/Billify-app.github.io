Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Introduction

At Billify, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Bill images and data you scan
  • Categories and tags you create
  • Device information for app functionality
  • Usage data to improve our service

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide bill management services
  • Sync your data across devices via iCloud
  • Improve our OCR and scanning features
  • Send important updates about the app

Data Security

We implement strong security measures:

  • Bank-level encryption for all data
  • Additional PIN protection for vault items
  • Secure iCloud sync using Apple's infrastructure
  • Regular security audits and updates

Data Storage

All your bills and data are stored:

  • Locally on your device
  • In your personal iCloud account (if enabled)
  • We do not store your data on our servers

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your data from the app
  • Opt-out of analytics
  • Request information about your data

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

app.billify@gmail.com

This privacy policy is subject to change. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.
