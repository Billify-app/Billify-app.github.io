Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Billify ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information Collection and Use

Personal Data

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information about you unless you voluntarily provide it to us
  • Your bills, receipts, and financial data remain private and are stored locally on your device
  • If enabled, data is synced through your personal iCloud account

Required Permissions

  • Camera: Required for scanning bills and receipts
  • Photo Library: Optional, for importing existing bill images
  • Face ID/Touch ID: Optional, for securing the vault feature
  • iCloud: Optional, for data synchronization across devices

Usage Data

  • App functionality and crash reports through Apple's standard reporting
  • Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance
  • No third-party analytics tools are used

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure encryption
  • Sensitive information in the vault is protected with additional encryption
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data

iCloud Integration

  • Data sync uses Apple's secure iCloud infrastructure
  • All data is encrypted during transmission
  • Your iCloud data remains private and is not accessible to us
  • Sync can be disabled at any time in app settings

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following Apple services:

  • iCloud for data synchronization
  • Apple's CoreML for text recognition
  • Apple's authentication services for biometric security

We do not share your data with any third-party services or advertisers.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your data remains on your device until you choose to delete it
  • Deleting the app removes all local data
  • iCloud data can be removed through your iCloud settings
  • You can request data deletion by contacting us

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and control your personal data
  • Delete your data from the app and iCloud
  • Opt-out of iCloud synchronization
  • Manage app permissions through your device settings
  • Request information about your data usage

Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Compliance with Laws

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Apple's App Store Review Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • App privacy best practices
