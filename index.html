FAQ

Common Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions about Billify

Is my data private?

Yes! Your bills are stored privately in your personal storage space. We use standard security practices and never share your information with third parties.

How accurate is the scanning feature?

Our AI-powered OCR technology achieves 98% accuracy in text recognition. The app automatically detects key information like dates, amounts, and categories, making the process quick and reliable.

Can I export my data?

Yes! You can export your bills as PDF files. This makes it easy to share your bills or keep backups for your records.

How does the iCloud sync work?

iCloud sync happens automatically in the background. Once enabled, your bills and categories are synced across all your iOS devices instantly. Changes made on one device are reflected everywhere.

What types of bills can I scan?

You can scan any type of bill or receipt! This includes utility bills, shopping receipts, restaurant bills, invoices, and more. Our AI adapts to different formats and layouts.

Can I organize bills into categories?

Absolutely! You can create custom categories to organize your bills. The app also suggests categories automatically based on the bill content, making organization effortless.

Is there a limit to how many bills I can store?

No limits! Store as many bills as you want. The app is optimized to handle large numbers of bills without affecting performance.

Do you offer customer support?

Yes! We provide customer support through email and in-app chat. Our team typically responds within 24 hours to help you with any questions or issues.
